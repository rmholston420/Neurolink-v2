# Muse Athena research notes

- Official Muse material says Muse S Athena combines EEG and fNIRS, adds Bluetooth LE 5.3, and keeps SDK support.
- BrainFlow supports Muse S Athena as `MUSE_S_ATHENA_BOARD` with default `preset=p1041;low_latency=true`.
- Useful upstream code: `muse-lsl`, `athena-tmr-lucid`, `neuralise_app`, and BrainFlow Athena examples.
